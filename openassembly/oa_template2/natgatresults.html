National Gathering Online Visioning Proposals Online Component

Below is the list of proposals created on July 4th as part of the online visioning process. These proposals are ranked in no particular order and each equally deserves the attention of the National Occupy movement. Visions created by Occupiers in Philadelphia will be posted on the National Gathering Visioning Group to allow internet users to express feedback over those visions. As we refine our visions and goals we can go forward with new organizational tools such as Open Assembly to share what knowledge we have gained in our actions and make collective decisions about those actions.

  • How can we build stronger occupy institutions?
  • Decentralized National GA

  • A Production Plan for Humanity
